Report II · Method

Positive above.
Negative below.

The flip is the single price at which the machine reverses polarity. Above it, dealer books absorb volatility; ranges compress and rallies die on approach. Below it, the same books chase price; a 1% candle prints before the coffee is cold.

"When the market is above the flip, hedge slower. When it is below, hedge sooner. There is no third instruction." — Vol. II, § 4.

ZeroGEX draws this line, every fifteen seconds, from the option chain that is currently printing — not from a stale close, not from a proxy.
