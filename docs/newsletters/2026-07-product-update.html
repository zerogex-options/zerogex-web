  A few questions I've been getting

How do I switch my plan (tier or monthly/annual)?

Open the Account page and click Manage subscription to open the Stripe billing portal — you can move between Basic and Pro and between monthly and annual right there. Upgrades (and monthly → annual) take effect immediately; downgrades and annual → monthly take effect at the end of your current period, so you keep what you paid for until then. Proration is applied and shows up on your next invoice, not as an upfront charge. If you're still in your free trial, switching keeps the trial — you won't be charged until it ends, and then you're billed the new plan's rate.

What do I get with Pro versus the free dashboard?

The live dashboard is always free. Paid plans unlock the full toolset — deeper GEX and flow breakdowns, trading signals, backtesting, and the Live Bulletin — and Pro adds programmatic API access for pulling the data yourself.

Which tickers do you cover?

SPY, SPX, and QQQ today, with the same read across all three for intraday index context. Expanding coverage is on the roadmap above.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes — one click from the billing portal on your Account page. You keep full access through the end of your current period; nothing is lost mid-cycle. And if cost is the issue, just reply — I'd rather find something that works than see you go.

Does ZeroGEX tell me what to trade?

No. It's built to give you better market-structure context before price gets there — it isn't financial advice, and no tool can promise an outcome. How you trade the levels is always your call.
