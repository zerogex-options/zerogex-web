## Notes on posting

- **Time of day**: weekday mornings (08:00–10:00 ET) for the announcement,
  weekend midday for commemorative posts when feed competition is lower.
- **Link-in-reply trick**: X downranks posts with outbound links in the
  primary tweet. For the lead announcement, put the link in the first reply,
  not the post itself.
- **Tag check**: confirm @FoldsOfHonor is the correct handle (verify on
  foldsofhonor.org before posting) — they may go by @foldsofhonor1.
- **Brand-use**: if we have an approved Folds of Honor partner-mark image,
  attach it. If not, post text-only — don't lift the unofficial logo.
- **Quote-reply / cross-post**: cross-post the lead announcement to LinkedIn
  and to any trading-community Discord we're active in. Don't auto-cross to
  every channel — adapt the framing to each.
